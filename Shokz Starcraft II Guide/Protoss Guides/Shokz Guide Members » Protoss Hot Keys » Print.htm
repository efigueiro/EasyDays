Protoss Hot Keys

Posted By Shokz On May 23, 2010 @ 5:57 am In Protoss Guides | No Comments

Units

Hotkeys to create units.

—–Protoss—–
In parentheses, the building or ability that creates the unit
Probe – E (Nexus)
Zealot – Z (Gateway, Warpgate)
Sentry – E (Gateway, Warpgate)
Stalker – S (Gateway, Warpgate)
Observer – B (Robotics Facility)
Immortal – I (Robotics Facility)
Warp Prism – A (Robotics Facility)
Colossus – C (Robotics Facility)
Phoenix – X (Stargate)
Void Ray – V (Stargate)
High Templar – T (Gateway, Warpgate)
Dark Templar – D (Gateway, Warpgate)
Archon – C (High Templar, Dark Templar morph)
Carrier – C (Stargate)
Interceptor – I (Carrier ability)
Mothership – M (Nexus)

Unit Abilities

Hotkeys to tell units to do stuff.

—–Protoss—–
Charge – C (Zealot)
Force Field (F) (Sentry)
Guardian Shield (G) (Sentry)
Hallucination (C) (Sentry)
Blink – B (Stalker)
Phasing Mode – E (Warp Prism)
Transport Mode – T (Warp Prism)
Graviton Beam – G (Phoenix)
Feedback – F (High Templar)
Psionic Storm – T (High Templar)
Archon Warp – C (High Templar, Dark Templar)
Train Interceptors – I (Carrier)
Mass Recall – R (Mothership)
Vortex – V (Mothership)

Templar Archives – T
Dark Shrine – D
Robotics Bay – B
Fleet Beacon – F

Building Abilities

Hotkeys to tell buildings to do stuff.

—–Protoss—–

Transform to Warpgate – G (Gateway)
Transform to Gateway – G (Warpgate)
