Sentry Micro Guide

Posted By Shokz On May 21, 2010 @ 4:44 pm In Protoss Guides | No Comments

The Sentry is a great Protoss support unit and it has many functions in the Protoss army. The Sentry has three skills which can change the turn out of any fight.

Force Field [F]

Force Field is one of the most powerful abilities, it allows you to control the movement flow and path of your opponents army. Force Field has numerous uses such as blocking off the entrance of your base when your opponent is about to attack, or if you get inside the opponents base while their army is outside you can block off their entrance to keep them out of their base while your army destroys it.

One of the best uses of Force Field is to use it in battle to minipulate your opponents forces. If your opponent is looking to run after a battle you can build a wall of Force Fields behind him and trap him in, or when you are in battle cast a wall of Force Fields in the middle of their army splitting his army in two giving you the advantage.
