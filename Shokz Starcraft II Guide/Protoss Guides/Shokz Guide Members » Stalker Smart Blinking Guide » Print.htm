Stalker Smart Blinking Guide

Posted By Shokz On May 18, 2010 @ 4:53 pm In Protoss Guides | No Comments

The Stalker Blink ability is an ability when mastered can become very useful. It requires good micro and strategy to use it in battle to your advantage.

Blink Ability

The Blink Ability for the stalker must be researched from the Twilight Council it cost 150 minerals and 150 gas and takes 110 seconds to research, so make sure you use chrono boost to speed up the training time.

Once Researched your able to use the hot key (B) and blink a stalker or a group of stalkers a short distance with a 10 second cooldown. This ability comes in a lot as your able to blink in and out of your opponents base and harass their minerals or blink on top of a range unit to gain the advantage.

Smart Blinking

When you have a large group of stalkers and you try to blink into your opponents base or onto a cliff, a lot of the times only half of the stalkers will make it since their won’t be room for the rest of the stalkers. Although using shift command and the right micro you can que your stalkers to blink onto a cliff and continue to walk forward so that the rest of your stalkers can also blink. This is extremely useful when trying to get your entire force into your opponents base for an attack.

Here is the 3 step way to do it

1.  Make your stalkers go where you want to blink from

2.  While holding shift, press blink to the destination

3.  Still holding shift make them go a bit further so there is room for all stalkers to blink.

Blink Attack Micro

Besides using blink to move into your opponents base blink can also be used with good Micro during the a battle. The video below shows the Protoss player winning against IdrA one of the top Starcraft 2 players by out Microing him with an amazing Stalker blink micro.

To use this strategy when your Stalkers are in combat you will want to click on a stalker in the front line with low HP and quickly blink them to the back of army (Make sure to you use the blink hot key “B”). This will allow you to keep your stalkers alive longer so they can continue to fight.
