Protoss Base Wall off

Posted By Shokz On May 27, 2010 @ 10:59 pm In Protoss Guides | No Comments

When you hear of players walling their base off they are usually referring to a Terran player walling off their base. Although as a Protoss player this is something you may want to do against Zerg players. The reason for doing so is a lot of times Zerg players will get speedlings and try to run them right past your starting units into your mineral line and destroy your probes.

Walling off your base as Protoss will be very different compared to walling off your base as Terran. Since Terran is able to raise and lower supply depot they can make a sealed tight wall off, where Protoss you will want to wall off the entire section of your ramp except for one grid where one unit at a time can walk through.

Once you wall off the ramp, produce a zealot and put him only gap in the wall and press “H” to tell him to hold position so he doesn’t try to chase after zerglings.

Even though your opponent may have more zerglings then you do zealots they won’t be able to get a surround on him and have to attack the zealot 1 on 1, which the zealot will be able to go destroy the zerglings as you continue to produce more units.
