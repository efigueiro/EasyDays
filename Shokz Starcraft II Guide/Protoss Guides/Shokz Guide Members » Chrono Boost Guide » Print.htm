Chrono Boost Guide

Posted By Shokz On June 28, 2010 @ 11:37 pm In Protoss Guides | No Comments

Chrono Boost is a Protoss ability that increases production / construction of units and buildings by 50%. Chrono Boost is casted from your Nexus and costs 25 energy and lasts for 20 seconds. Chrono Boost is a key component in having a good Protoss Macro and if used correctly will give you a key advantage in all your games.

How to quickly use Chrono Boost

To use Chrono boost correctly you must learn to use it quickly and efficiently. At the start of each game I always Hot key my Nexus to the control group “5″, this allows me to quickly select the Nexus by clicking 5, or be taken directly to my Nexus by double clicking 5. Once you have the Nexus selected hit “C” to select Chrono Boost which can then be casted on any building that you want.

It is very important to get this down so you can quickly cast Chrono boost even while you are in battle or just Macroing in your base.

Knowing when to use Chrono Boost

The Nexus has 100 energy and Chrono Boost cost 25 to cast, so with a full energy Nexus your able to cast Chrono boost 4 times. This makes it important to not waste your Chrono Boosts and to use them effectively. At the start of the game when you reach your first 25 energy it’s always good to cast Chrono Boost on your nexus to speed up the production of your probes, unless you are going for an early rush you can save the Chrono Boost to cast on your Gateway to quickly produce a Zealot.

Chrono Boost Tips

  • You can cast Chrono Boost on Warp Gates to decrease Warp cooldown time
  • You can cast Chrono Boost on buildings being produce to speed up production
