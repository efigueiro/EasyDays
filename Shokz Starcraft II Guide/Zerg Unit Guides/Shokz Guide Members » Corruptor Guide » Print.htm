Corruptor Guide

Posted By Shokz On July 27, 2010 @ 1:05 am In Zerg Unit Guides | Comments Disabled

Race Weak Against Okay Against Strong Against
Zerg Hydralisks Mutalisks Brood Lord, Overseer
Protoss Stalker Archon, High Templar, Sentry Carrier, Colossus, Mothership
Terran Marine, Thor Viking Banshee, Battlecruiser

Corruptors are the Zerg’s main anti-air unit. They are very powerful against any air but they cannot attack ground units making the the main choice if your opponent starts to mass a force of air units. The other purpose of Corruptors is you can morph them into Brood Lords who are some of the most powerful units in the game and when combined with Corruptors make for a insane combination.

Skills and Strategies

Corruption: Covers a target in acid causing it to take more damage. This is a great skill although I don’t see it used very often, when you are attacking an opponents base with your forces this works great to cast on a couple buildings to quickly take down once you have take out your opponents air. This also works great if you ever want to run in and try to snipe out an opponent’s expansion. Just create one Corruptor with your ground forces and cast Corruption on their expansion focus fire it down and then run out of there.

Against other Races

Vs Protoss: Corruptors along with Hdyralisks are the best defense against Void Rays if your opponent is massing them building a couple Corruptors will help easily dispose of them. The main use of Corruptors when playing against a Protoss player is when you see they are going Carriers, they are one of the only units that can effectively hold up against them and take them out.

Vs Terran: When your opponent tries to Banshee rush you Corruptors are the quickest way expose of them, Hydralisks also work well but for a lot of players who are going into a Mutalisk tech then going Corruptors for even better anti-air then Mutalisks is a good way to go.

Vs Zerg: The only reason to go Corruptors in a Zerg vs Zerg match up is to get Brood Lords late in the game or to counter Brood Lords. Brood Lords can be one of the most devastating units and with their long range can take ground units out quickly, but Corruptors can easily counter them and take them out of the sky with no problem and quicker then Mutalisks can.
