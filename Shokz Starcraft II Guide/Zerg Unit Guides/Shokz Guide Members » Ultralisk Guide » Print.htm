Ultralisk Guide

Posted By Shokz On July 24, 2010 @ 2:51 am In Zerg Unit Guides | Comments Disabled

Race Weak Against Okay Against Strong Against
Zerg Brood Lord, Mutalisk, Roach Baneling, Hydralisk Zergling
Protoss Carrier, Immortal, Mothership, Void Ray, Archon Colossus, Dark Templar, High Templar, Sentry, Stalker Zealot
Terran Banshee, Battlecruiser, Marauder, Siege Tank Ghost, Thor, Viking Hellion, Marine, Reaper

Oh, the mighty Ultralisk, these big heavy units are the top end of the Zerg army and one of the most powerful ground units in the game. In order to build Ultralisks you must first have a Hive and then make a Ultralisk Cavern, this makes Ultralisks a rare sight in 1v1 games since most games do not go that far into late game.

The Ultralisk is an armored unit that deals splash damage to ground units and extra damage to buildings, they work great when dealing with a mass of lower tier units although if caught out in the open they can easily be surrounded and taken down quickly.

Skills and Strategies

Chitinous Plating: Increases the armor of all Ultralisks by +2. If you plan on making Ultralisks they are a very large investment and you should invest in the increased armor, it will help overall when taking the brute force of the damage while your other forces continue to attack.

Against other Races

Vs Protoss: Ultralisks are very strong against Zealots or Zealot / Stalker builds although you have to watch out for for Immortals since they do bonus damage against armored units like the Ultralisks they can take them out pretty quickly. Once you get Tier 3 your best bet is to go Brood Lords over Ultralisks when playing against Protoss.

Vs Terran: Ultralisks can take out the Marine Infantry very quickly with their splash damage and work very well if you mix them with Zerglings to counter mass Marauders, as long as they stay to a Tech / Infantry build then Ultralisks work will against Terran.

Vs Zerg: I tend to stay away from Ultralisks while playing another Zerg player since most units can counter them pretty easily at a fraction of the price. Ultralisks are good if your opponent is trying to overwhelm you with mass Zerglings at the ladder part of the game and can hold their own against Hydralisks but in most cases I go with different units.
