Mutalisk Guide

Posted By Shokz On July 23, 2010 @ 11:20 pm In Zerg Unit Guides | Comments Disabled

Race Weak Against Okay Against Strong Against
Zerg Corruptor, Hydralisk Baneling, Brood Lord, Roach, Ultralisk, Zergling
Protoss Carrier, Archon, High Templar, Phoenix, Sentry, Stalker Mothership, Void Ray Colossus, Dark Templar, Immortal, Zealot
Terran Battlecruiser, Marine, Viking, Thor, Viking Banshee, Hellion, Marauder, Reaper, Siege Tank

Mutalisks are Zergs most balanced air unit with the ability to attack both air and land. Each time a Mutalisks attack it will bounce to 2 additonal targets dealing a 3rd of the damage than the previous hit. They Mutalisks speed makes it one of the best harassing units in the game and if your able to get them quick you can catch your opponent off guard with a Mutalisk rush and win the game or severely damage his economy.

Against other Race

Vs Protoss: Stalkers are a very strong counter to Mutalisks, and Stalkers are in most Protoss player builds which means that you need to use Mutalisks more as a harassing unit against most Protoss players by attacking their minerals and flying away once their forces come to defend. If your opponent masses stalkers once he sees that you are going Mutalisks you can build a Zerglings with a combination of Mutalisks to take out the Stalkers.

Vs Terran: Against a player who is pushing a heavy Marauder build teching into Mutalisks is a great counter to mass Marauders. Terran has a lot of good anti-air defense in their arsenal, first off Turrets are very powerful against Mutalisks and unless you have a group of Mutalisks and there is only 1 or two Turrets I would stay away from them. Marines are also a good counter to Mutalisks but cannot win a game with just mass marines, so make sure you continue producing Zerglings so it will force your opponent to spend money on Hellions to counter the Zerlings.

When going against Thors you want to make sure you spread your mutalisks out, Thors do splash damage and if you keep your Mutalisks close together they will be able to do extra damage with Splash. When you are attacking Thor or two with Muta’s spread them out to limit the damage each one takes and you’ll be able to take them out.

Vs Zerg: Mutalisks are good against Zerg if you can get them out before they can tech to Hydralisks, once they are able to produce Hydralisks they can counter the Mutalisks pretty easily, although that doesn’t mean you still can’t use them against Hydralisks, by creating a couple of Mutalisks but still doing your normal build against Hydralisks you can use your Mutalisks to attack his base while you engage in battle with your opponent.
