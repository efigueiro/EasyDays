Baneling Guide

Posted By Shokz On July 21, 2010 @ 10:36 pm In Zerg Unit Guides | Comments Disabled

Race Weak Against Okay Against Strong Against
Zerg Brood Lord, Mutalisk, Roach, Ultralisk Hydralisk Zergling
Protoss Carrier, Mothership, Void Ray, Colossus, Immortal, Phoenix, Stalker Dark Templar, High Templar, Sentry Zealot
Terran Banshee, Battlecruiser Siege Tank, Thor, Viking Marine, Marauder

Banelings are suicidal units that explode and do a huge amount of splash damage, they are great against clusters of units such as Marine / Marauders, zealots and Stalkers / or Hydras and Zerlings. Banelings are made by morphing Zerglings into Banelings and require a Banelings nest which allows your opponent to see that you are going Banelings with early scouting.

Skills and Strategies

Centrifugal Hooks: This upgrade from the Banelings nest increases the Banelings movement speed which is very helpful when doing a Baneling rush since your opponent won’t have enough time to focus fire your Banelings before the reach him.

  • Breaking Terran Wall with Baneling Guide
  • Banelings have the ability to explode while burrowed, this allows you to place Banelings in strategic areas and wait for your opponent to walk over them and then have them explode on your opponent.
  • If you can get a couple of Banelings into your opponents mineral line they will be able to explode taking out most of their workers.
  • You can do a Baneling drop on your opponents army by filling up a couple Overlords with Banelings and droping them on your opponent when you engage him in battle.

Against other Races

Vs Protoss: Banelings work well if your opponent is Zealot rushing you or massing Zealots later in the game, although they are not very strong against many other Protoss units since they can focus fire the Banelings before you get to them. If your able to do a Baneling Drop on the Probes you’ll really harm his economy although you must first tech to a Liar in order to get Overlord transport.

Vs Terran:Banelings work great against Marine / Marauder balls if you can get a couple of Banelings to explode on a tightly packed group of Marines and Marauders you should easily be able to finish them off with the rest of your forces. Banelings are also very important against Terran if you plan on doing a Zergling rush as you will to use Banelings to break the Terran wall to rush in your Zerglings

Vs Zerg: A lot of times in Zerg matches early rushes will turn into Zergling micro wars all about who can produce the most Zerglings and Micro them the best to win, but by tossing a few Banelings into the mix your able to try and take out large groups of Zerglings giving you the advantage. Banelings also work well later in the game against Hydralisks, but should be avoided if your opponent is massing Roaches.
