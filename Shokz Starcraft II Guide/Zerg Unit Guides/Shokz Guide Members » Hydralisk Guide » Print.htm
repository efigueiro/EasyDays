Hydralisk Guide

Posted By Shokz On July 22, 2010 @ 10:25 pm In Zerg Unit Guides | Comments Disabled

Race Weak Against Okay Against Strong Against
Zerg Ultralisk, Zergling Baneling Brood Lord, Roach Corruptor, Mutalisk
Protoss Archon, Colossus, High Templar, Zealot Carrier, Dark Templar, Immortal, Mothership, Sentry, Stalker Phoenix, Void Ray
Terran Hellion, Siege Tank Banshee, Battlecruiser, Ghost, Raven, Reaper, Thor, Viking Marauder, Marine

Hydralisks is the Zerg’s first real anti-air unit and an all around good unit that can hold it’s own on the ground and defend against air attacks well. Hydras are not as strong as they use to be in the original Starcraft and work better in a mixture of units than just massing them. A good combination is going straight from a Zergling rush into Hydralisks while still producing Zerglings or into a Roach / Hydra build which works well as the Roaches provide the meat up front while the Hydralisks can attack from behind the roaches.

Skills and Strategies

Grooved Spines: This is upgraded from the Hydralisk Den and increases the Hydralisks attack range, this is a great upgrade if you are going to go any type of Hydralisks build, it will help against air since your Hydralisks can attack from farther away and also in battle.

  • Hydralisks are a great support unit and work well with almost any Zerg combination of units.

Hydralisk/Zergling Combination
The Hydralisk’s primary role within this combo is for early air-tech support. If this does not apply, then the Hydralisk will be there for general support. The Hydralisk should be at the back of this force while the Zerglings go in for close combat. It is suggested that you upgrade the Hydralisk speed and carapace, because the Zergling and Hydralisk share this upgrade.

Hydralisk/Roach Combination
Like the Zergling combo, the Hydralisk will accompany the Roaches in case your opponent decides to fast tech to air. Depending on how you use your Roaches, that Hydralisks might be included in more combat than just support for the Roaches.

Hydralisk/Mutalisk Combination
This possibly the most flexible combo within the Zerg ****nal. Both the Hydralisk and Mutalisk can both rely on each other for support. Your Hydralisks might take the majority of the damage depending on whether you micro the Mutalisks or not. It is suggested that you upgrade Hydralisk speed, but you may have to pool more time into upgrading carapace or attacks.

Hydralisk/Corruptor Combination
This combination is more of a direct approach than other combos and should be used if your opponent chooses to tech and build air units. This will probably be a more popular choice than other Hydralisk combos, mainly because it occurs when your opponent techs to air. Hydralisks are more useful against armored units, which consists of the Terran’s most common air unit, the Viking. Hydralisks will also be used to support against ground units.

Against other Races

Vs Protoss: Hydralisks combined with Zerglings or Roaches are a great counter to Zealot / Stalker combos and overall work very well against almost any unit combination. Hydralisks are also the best counter to Void Rays, if you see that your opponent is massing Void Rays then quickly producing Hydralisks will be the best bet. You can always go Corruptors against Void Rays although this leave you with out a ground attacking unit and Hydra’s are very strong against Void Rays so they are the best way to go.

Vs Terran: Against Terran Hydralisks work well but must be paired again with either Roaches or Zerglings since Stimed Marauders can can take them out pretty quickly and Siege Tanks love to tear apart groups of Hydralisks unless you can get some units close enough to focus fire them down. Hydralisks are the best counter to Banshee rushes, a lot of players like to get cloaked Banshees and having a Overseer and a couple Hydralisks will be the best counter to any type of Banshee build or rush.

Vs Zerg: Hydralisk work great when playing against other Zerg players, if you have enough of them they can take out Zerglings rather quickly and grouped with Zerglings are great against Roaches. A lot of players like to go Zergling to Mutalisks and the best way to counter this is massing Hydralisks, they will be the best counter Mutalisks and you can use Zergligns to harass their base while protecting yours.
