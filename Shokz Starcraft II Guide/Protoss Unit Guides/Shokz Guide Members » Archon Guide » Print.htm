Archon Guide

Posted By Shokz On July 19, 2010 @ 6:02 pm In Protoss unit Guides | No Comments

Race Weak Against Okay Against Strong Against
Zerg Brood Lord Hydralisk, Infestor, Ultralisk Baneling, Corruptor, Mutalisk, Roach
Protoss Phoenix, Carrier, Colossus Dark Templar, High Templar, Immortal, Mothership, Sentry, Stalker, Void Ray Zealot
Terran Battlecruiser, Ghost, Thor Banhsee, Marauder, Raven, Reaper, Siege Tank, Viking Hellion, Marine

Archons are made by merging two Templars together. You can combine either two High Templars or 2 Dark Templars or one of each to build a Archon, if you plan on making Archons with out using the Templars first than the cheapest way to go is two High Templars.

  • 2 High Templars: 250 Minerals / 250 Gas
  • 2 Dark Templars: 100 Minerals / 300 Gas
  • 1 High Templar, 1 High Templar: 175 minerals / 275 Gas

Archons are not as strong as they were in the original Starcraft and are meant more of a last resort unit that you would create after you use all your High Templars energy. I only get Archons after I go a High Templar based build and once I use all the energy of the High Templars in battle I than morph them into Archons to provide addtional attack power.

Archons have the ability to attack both ground and air and deal splash damage with all of their attacks so in a group of 2 or 3 with units to back them up they can be an effective unit, they are generally strong against melee units.
