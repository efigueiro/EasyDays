Dark Templar Guide

Posted By Shokz On July 19, 2010 @ 6:00 pm In Protoss unit Guides | No Comments

Race Weak Against Okay Against Strong Against
Zerg Overseer, Brood Lord, Mutalisk, Baneling, Hydralisk Queen, Roach, Ultralisk Zergling
Protoss Carrier, Mothership, Void Ray, Phoenix Archon, Colossus, Stalker, Zealot, Immortal Sentry, High Templar
Terran Banshee, Battlecruiser, Hellion Ghost, Reaper, Siege Tank, Marine, Viking Marauder, Thor

Dark Templars are a cloaked unit which deals a large amount of damage, although if detected can be killed very easily. You don’t see Dark Templars often in higher level games since most players in Platinum / Diamond league will easily be able to defend against them, although in the lower level leagues this is something you have to watch out for in late games as a lot of low level protoss players like to try to tech to Dark Templars.

I generally would not recommend ever teching right to Dark Templars since if your opponent is able to counter them then you will be very far behind in the game, although they do work well if you mix in a couple in your main army since they can take out other ground units very quickly if they go undetected.

If you do go Dark Templars make sure that you hide the Dark Templar shrine in the back of your base, or even outside of your base on the edge of the map so that your opponent doesn’t see it. If your opponent sees the DT Shrine then they will quickly get detectors making your Dark Templars useless.

Against other Races

Vs Protoss: Dark Templars are tough to pull off against Protoss since they can easily get an observer to fly anywhere on the map. In lower level leagues you also see a lot of Protoss players placing a Photon Cannon or two at the entrance of their base. A good way to get into their base with a couple of Dark Templars is to use a Warp Prism and place it down in the back of your opponents base and warp them.

Vs Terran: Terran players have the ability to scan anywhere on the map revealing any cloaked units, so instead of using Dark Templars to attack your opponents army you can take a couple of them straight into their mineral line and have them 1 hit their workers. This will help to damage their economy and once they scan you can run away from the scan area, to then go back and harass again.

Vs Zerg: The main detection Zerg will have to reveal Dark Templars are Overseers which require a Liar, it’s not likely that you’ll be able to get Dark Templars out before the Zerg upgrades to a Liar so if you do go Dark Templars against Zerg the best thing to do is to wait till you have 4 -6 of them and go straight for their Liar and take it out. By the time the morph a Overlord to an Overseer your DTs will be able to take out the Liar and you can then work on the rest of his base.
