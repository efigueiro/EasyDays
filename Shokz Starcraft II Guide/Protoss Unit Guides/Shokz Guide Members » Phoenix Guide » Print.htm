Phoenix Guide

Posted By Shokz On July 20, 2010 @ 4:30 pm In Protoss unit Guides | No Comments

Race Weak Against Okay Against Strong Against
Zerg Hydralisk, Corruptor Queen Brood Lord, Mutalisk, Overseer
Protoss Stalker, Carrier Archon, Mothership, Void Ray Colossus, Immortal, Zealot, Dark Templar, High Templar
Terran Marine, Viking, Thor Battlecruiser Banshee, Siege Tank, Marauder, Hellion

The Phoenix is a light weight flying unit which can attack while on the move. Their attack isn’t very strong and other than killing Overlords I don’t really use them as a attack unit, they do have one ability Graviton Beam that makes them useful and if you use them correctly can be pretty powerful.

Skills and Strategies

Graviton Beam: Makes the target unit float into the air disabling its abilities. The effect last up to 10 seconds and cannot be used on massive units. This skill is the one of the only reasons to make Phoenixes, it works great if you want to harass your opponent, you can take a couple of them to their mineral line and Graviton Beam their workers 1 by 1 allowing your other Phoenixes to kill them once you pull them up into the air. This is also a very useful skill to disable special units during battle such as ghost, or powerful units such as Immortals.

  • Use Graviton Beam to harass opponents workers
  • You can Graviton a Zerg player’s Queen while other Phoenixes kill her
  • Phoenixes can attack while moving so you can kite other air units by constantly moving around them while attacking

Against other Races

Vs Protoss: One of the best uses of Phoenixes when playing against another Protoss is when they go Immortals, you can use the Phoenixes to lift the Immortals in the air for 10 seconds during battle leaving them useless while your is attacking theirs.

Vs Terran: Just like with Protoss you can use the Phoenixes to Graviton Beam battle changing opponents such as Siege Tanks and Ghost. Removing Siege Tanks form a battle will give you a huge advantage in the fight and will give you time to kill their forces before the Siege tanks can even attack. You can also use Phoenixes to harass your opponent by beaming up their mules and having other Phoenixes shoot them down, hurting their economy.

Vs Zerg: Phoenixes are an alright counter to Mutalisks although I prefer to go Stalkers with blink if my opponent is going to mass Mutalisks. They do work great though harassing the Zergs base, you can beam up the Zerg’s Queen while your other Phoenixes kill her and then start taking out their drones. If you plan on going Phoenixes against Zerg use them mainly as harassing units and make sure you put most of your resources into your army.
