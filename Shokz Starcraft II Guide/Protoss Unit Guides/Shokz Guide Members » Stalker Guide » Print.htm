Stalker Guide

Posted By Shokz On July 19, 2010 @ 3:02 am In Protoss unit Guides | No Comments

Race Weak Against Okay Against Strong Against
Zerg Zergling Baneling, Broodlord, Hydralisk, Infestor, , Overeer, Queen, Roach, Ultralisk Mutalisk, Corruptor
Protoss Immortal, Dark Templar, Zealot Archon, Carrier, Colossus, High Templar, Phoenix, Sentry, Void Ray
Terran Marauder, Marine, Siege Tank Ghost, Raven, Thor, Battlecruiser Banshee, Hellion, Viking, Reaper

Stalkers are the replacement of the Dragoon from the original Starcraft, they are a ranged unit that does well in numbers and can be used to harass your opponent through the game with their upgraded Blink ability. Stalkers are also one of Protoss’s strongest anti-air unit and should be used in almost all cases when in need of anti-air and dealing with any type of air / ranged unit.

Skills and Strategies

Blink: is an upgraded that is researched from the Twilight Council that gives the Stalker the ability to teleport to a nearby location, with a 10 second cool down. When ever you plan to go a heavy Stalker build you should always research Blink as it can be very useful when you learn how to use it correctly. You can use blink to quickly close the gap between range units and most importantly air units, by Blinking under an air attack such as Banshees or Void Rays allow all your stalkers to get in range to attack the air units instantly rather then having to walk in range while being attacked.

A very useful strategy with blink is base harassing, as long as you have sight of a ledge you can blink into your opponents base from the sides or the back and attack his harvesters, than once his units come to defend them you can blink out of the base. You can continue to keep up this pressure while you build up your force and are ready to attack, this also works well in harassing their expansions and trying to take them out of their game psychologically.

  • When facing a Terran with Siege Tanks Blink next to his Siege tanks to quickly take them out.
  • Against Brood Lords Blink underneath them putting you in range to attack them directly
  • Use a couple of Stalkers with Blink to harass our opponents expansion and Main’s economy

Against other Races

Vs Protoss: Stalkers work well against Protoss if you mix them in with some Zealots, you don’t want to just go Stalkers unless you see that your opponent is teching to Void Rays, a lot of Protoss players will try to build mass Void Rays to win which will be easily countered by Stalkers. Stalkers with Immortals / Collossus work very well together also and if your opponent is getting a lot of Stalkers then Immortals will help easily take them out.

Vs Terran: Stalkers are a great choice against almost any Terran player, if they are going a with a Marine and Marauder Ball then a mix of Stalkers, Zealots, Immortals, and Colossus will be the best counter. If they go a Mech build with Siege Tanks and Thors you can use blink to quickly get on top of the Siege tanks and then destroy the Thors, and since Terran has the strongest air force in the game Stalkers and Phoenixes will be the best option to counter any air units such as Banshee and Battlecruisers.

Vs Zerg: In early game against Zerg I try to get a lot of Zealots, compared to stalkers till you can see what the Zerg player is going since Zerglings can easily surround stalkers. Once you see his build if he is going into a Roach / Hydra based build then Stalkers with Immortals will be the best counter or if he does a Mutalisk rush / Tech then Stalkers and Phoenixes will counter the Mutalisks, you can also use Blink to get close to Mutas when they are attacking / fleeing from your base.
