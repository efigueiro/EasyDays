Immortal Guide

Posted By Shokz On July 20, 2010 @ 4:39 am In Protoss unit Guides | No Comments

Race Weak Against Okay Against Strong Against
Zerg Brood Lord, Mutalisk, Zergling, Hydralisk Baneling Roach, Queen, Ultralisk
Protoss Carrier, Mothership, Phoenix, Void Ray, Dark Templar, Zealot Archon, High Templar, Sentry Stalker, Colossus
Terran Battlecruiser, Banshee, Ghost, Marine Hellion, Reaper Marauder, Siege Tank, Thor, Viking

Immortals are an armored unit which do an insane amount of damage to armored units and buildings. They have a Hardened Shields that reduce damage to their shields from any heavy attack and provide great support to a Zealot and Stalker based army.

Skills and Strategies

Hardened Shield: The Immortal’s Hardened Shields reduce the damage of incoming high damage attacks to 10 if his shields are still up. This is a great ability, if your Immortals have their shields then any heavy damage will be reduce to only 10, for example if a Siege Tank hits an Immortal that has their shield up instead of doing their normal damage of 60 they will only do 10 damage to the Immortals shields. The same goes for roaches, instead of the normal 16 damage a Roach would do the shield will only take 10 damage per hit from a Roach.

Against other Races

Vs Protoss: Immortals are a great counter to a heavy stalker build, if your opponent is going mainly stalkers then going Immortals will take them out quickly, although you have to watch out for Zealots as they are a strong counter against Immortals. Immortals are also great against Colossus if your able to get in range of them, if your opponent goes Colossus and Stalkers Immortals will be the best counter, just make sure that you focus fire down the Colossus quickly.

Vs Terran: One of the hardest things to counter in the Terran army are well placed Siege Tanks, Immortals are great against them long as they have their shields. Siege Tanks only do 10 damage to the Immortals while the Immortals does extra damage to Siege Tanks taking them out very quickly. Along with Siege Tanks, Immortals are great counters to Marauders and provide great support when going against a Marine/Marauder ball. The thing you have to watch out for when playing against Terran are ghost, if they get an EMP off on your Immortals that will instantly destroy their shields leaving them vulnerable to attack.

Vs Zerg: Immortals are the easiest counter to Roaches, you don’t find many players going a very heavy roach army anymore due to all the nerfs they received but when a player does go roaches then Immortals will be the best choice to counter them. The Immortals weakness against Zerg are Zerglings, they are small and quick and can easily surround an Immortal taking him down quickly. If you are to go a Immortal build to counter roaches some Zealots so that they can handle any Zerglings that they throw at you.
