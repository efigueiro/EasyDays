Sentry Guide

Posted By Shokz On July 19, 2010 @ 5:59 pm In Protoss unit Guides | No Comments

Race Weak Against Okay Against Strong Against
Zerg Brood Lord, Baneling, Ultralisk, Zergling Hydralisk, Mutalisk, Queen, Roach Overlord
Protoss Archon, Colossus, Dark Templar, Mothership, Phoenix, Void Ray, Zealot Immortal, Stalker, Carrier
Terran Battlecruiser, Banshee, Ghost, Hellion, Reaper, Siege Tank, Thor Marauder, Marine, Viking

The Sentry’s is a Protoss support unit that have a set of great special abilities that when used correctly can give you the advantage in any battle. You can start producing Sentries from your Gateways as soon as you have a Cybernetics Core and for being such an early game unit they can be used through out the match into the end game as their skills make them useful in any situation.

Skills and Strategies

Force Field: Creates a force field that lasts for 15 seconds and is non-destructible wall. Force field only cost 50 energy allowing the Sentry to cast it as soon as the Sentry is built.

One of the most common uses of force field is to cast it on your base’s ramp to block any opponents from rushing into your base as you continue to tech. You can also use the same strategy if you attack your opponent and his units are out of his base, you can use force field to block off his ramp not allowing his units to enter his base while you destroy his structures.

As you become better with Sentries you can start using them in a group of 4 – 5 as support to your army, when you are able to engage in battle you can spam cast Force Field in the middle of your opponents army splitting them in half, so that half of his army is trapped behind the force field wall unable to attack while you kill the other half of his army.

Guardian Shield: Creates a ranged aura around the Sentry that reduces income ranged damage to friendly units by 2 and lasts for 15 seconds. This is a very useful ability and should be used often when you are about to engage in a battle with a heavy ranged based army, this ability doesn’t stack with one another, but it is a good idea to have a couple Sentries cast it if you have a large army so that all your units are inside the Shield.

Hallucination: Creates hallucinations that cannot deal damage, but last for 60 seconds and will be revealed by enemy detectors. A very common use of this skill is to hallucinate a Phoenix and then fly it over your opponents base, this is a great way to scout your opponents base with out wasting any minerals as it only cost you energy to produce the hallucination. It can also be used to duplicate your army to increase the size right before battle in hope that the Hallucinated units will be taking damage instead of the real units while they kill your opponents army.

Against other Races

Vs Protoss: When facing another Protoss, if you are teching to stalkers / immortals then Force Field will be the best option to block off the entrance of your base encase your opponent tries to Zealot rush you, since force field lasts for 15 seconds you should be able to continue to block off your entrance till you are ready to move out on him with your army. Guardian Shield will be helpful later on in the game if your opponent is going a heavy Stalker build or upgrades to Void Rays and even Carriers as the Shield will help reduce any damage by your opponent.

Vs Terran: Force Fields work great against Marine and Marauder balls, if you can do a Force Field split and trap half his army behind the Force Fields you will only have to deal with half of the Marines and Marauders at one time. I also recommend using Guardian Shield in almost any battle against Terran since every one of their units is a ranged based unit it will help reduce the damage dramatically when fighting against their armies.

Vs Zerg: A lot of Zerg players like to try to do a Speedling rush in early games against Protoss where they rush Speedlings into your base and attack your minerals, you can do a Protoss Wall Off or use Force Field to block your entrance not allowing the Speedlings into your base as you continue unit production.  Using Force Field to split up Hydras and Roaches during a battle also works very well although should be done around a choke point so that you use the least amount of energy possible.
