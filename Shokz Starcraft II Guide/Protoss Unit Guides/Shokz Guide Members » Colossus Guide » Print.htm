Colossus Guide

Colossus are straight out of War of the Worlds, these tall aliens are a powerful unit that has the ability to walk up and down cliffs making them a great support unit that when in the back of an army shooting down the rays of damage they can quickly take out units by the masses. Colossus work very well when they are in the back of your army shooting over them at your opponent, they are not the most armored unit and when focused fired upon they go down quickly.

Skills and Strategies

Cliff Walk: Colossus can walk up and down cliffs allowing them to quickly move across the map with out any obstacles getting in their way. This is makes the Colossus great harassment units on most maps you’ll be able to access your opponents main base or expansions from a cliff or ledge which you can use the colossus to get behind your their minerals and start taking out their workers and then once they bring their units over to defend their base you can walk your Colossus back over the cliff.

Extended Thermal Lances: This is an upgrade from the Robotics Facility which increases the range of your Colossus weapon. This is a great upgrade if your plan on using a heavy Colossus build since it will allow them to stand even further back when they are attacking, this will help reduce the threat of the Colossus being focused fired since they will have to walk through your main ground forces just to reach them, it is also helpful to allow Colossus to stand on a nearby ledge while in battle to stay out of range of enemy units.

Against other Races

Vs Protoss: When playing against another Protoss player Colossus work great since most Protoss armies are ground base. Colossus are very strong against Zealot and Stalker combos, the main thing you will want to watch out for are Immortals as they can take down a Colossus very quickly.

Vs Terran: Colossus will absolultey destroy Marine and Marauder balls, if your Terran player is going a very heavy infantry build with Marines and Marauders then a good sized ground units with a couple Colossus to back them up will be one of the best counters you can go. You need to watch out for Vikings, they have very long range and can shoot down the Colossus from a distance. If you see your opponent is getting Vikings to counter the Colossus then use Stalkers to focus fire them down before they can take out the Colossus.

Vs Zerg: Like with the other two races this will be dependent on what the Zerg player goes. If they go a quick Zergling into Mutalisks than stay away from Colossus as the Mutalisks will be a strong counter to them. If the Zerg opponent goes with a Hydra / Roach based army then Colossus backing up Stalkers / Zealots will be the best route. Upgraded with Thermal Lance they will be able to out range the Zerg units and take them down quickly with their splash damage.
