Breaking Terran Wall with Banelings

Posted By Shokz On June 6, 2010 @ 3:52 am In Zerg Guides | No Comments

When playing against Terran players they will almost always wall off  their base with units behind their wall to protect against speedling rushes. This makes it very difficult to rush to do a speedling rush against a Terran player as just zerglings wont’ be able to break through their wall if they use SCVs to repair their Supply Depots. This is where banglings come in which will allow you to break down the Terran wall and give you an open passage to send your speedlings into your opponents base.

Build Order

9 -- Overlord

12 -- Extractor

12 -- Spawning Pool

When going for the Banling Wall build you want to do a normal build but with a 10 or 12 spawning pool, the key is getting your gas before or the same time as your spawning pool since this is a gas heavy build and will need a lot of gas as quickly has possible. Once your extractor finishes make sure to put 3 drones gathering gas and start building a queen the moment your spawning pool is complete.

Once you have your queen cast Spawn Larva on your hatchery and start your zergling speed upgrade, along with a Banlings Nest. As your zergling upgrade is being trained and your banlings nest is being built continue to build zerglings so that you are ready to attack once the upgrade finishes.

Breaking down the Wall

Once the zergling speed upgrade is finished and your banlings nest is ready move your speedlings out on the map close to your opponents base. Make sure your back far enough from his base where he won’t see you and morph 4 -5 zerglings into banlings.

Take a speedling and run up your opponents ramp to see his wall layout and then bring it back down. Set your Speedlings to a 1 control group and your Banlings to another control group so you can quickly access them. When you are ready take your Banlings and run them up the ramp into a Supply Depot to break down the wall.

Once you’ve breached the wall quickly grab your Speedlings and rush them up into his base and take out his units and then head straight for his workers. If you have any additional Banlings left run them into his marines or marauders to quickly dispose of them as your Speedlings are running into his base. Continue to produce Zerglings while you are attacking and set the rally point to your opponents base so you have reinforcements on the way.

If you don’t win

Sometimes you’ll run across a player who is well prepared and can actually survive the Banling, Speedling attack. If your attack is unsuccessful and you don’t win the game from the attack then tech straight into Hdyras. Right before you attack it is a good idea to upgrade your Hatchery to a Liar so by the time your done attacking you can quickly build a hydralisk den and transition into normal macro mode.
