Zerg Creep Guide

Posted By Shokz On June 3, 2010 @ 1:23 am In Zerg Guides | No Comments

Creep is the one main thing that sets Zerg apart from the other races. Creep is the organic ground that serves as the nourisment and lifeblood of the Zerg structures and buildings and has a distinct advantage to Zerg players when used correctly.

Features of Creep

All Zerg buildings must be built on Creep, the exception to this rule is a Hatchery which can be built anywhere as this is needed to expand. While this may bee seen as a downfall it actually isn’t bad as Creep gives you a lot of benefits and using Creep to your advantage is crucial in each game.

Other races cannot build on Creep, this will prevent other races from building a bunker or proxy pylon right next to your hatchery, although other Zerg players can build on your creep and you can build on theirs.

Grants 30% movement speed bonus to all Zerg units. This is most beneficial part of creep, this is why players try to expand creep as much as possible along the map so their units can move along the map at a quicker rate. When you have a large portion of the map covered in creep the extra 30% bonus speed will allow you to defend your base against rushes easier, reinforce your army quicker, and escape from battles which you are out numbered or don’t have an advantage.

Spreading Creep

Creep Tumor

Since spreading creep along the map is so important for Zerg, knowing how to do it effectively is just as important. The most effective way to spread creep is by a Creep Tumor.

A Creep Tumor is casted by your Queen and is casted on existing creep and will generate creep growth around the creep tumor. You can then cast an additional Creep tumor from your original creep tumor to keep growing creep along the map and this process can be repeated creep tumor to creep tumor. Creep tumors are also cloaked, but can be killed if your opponent has a detector near the Creep Tumor.

Overlord Creep Generation

The second way to spreed Creep is from an Overlord, once you upgrade your Hatchery to a Liar all your overlords get the ability to generate Creep. This ability will allow the overlord to spill creep down on the ground in a single area, although the overlord must remain their or the creep will disappear.

Besides spreading your overlords over the map and creating a Creep highway to your enemies base you can use the generate creep feature to spill creep on all the expansion along the map which will prevent your opponent from expanding since they will not be able to build on the Creep.

Creep Highway

A Creep Highway is refereed to when a player creates a road of Creep from his base to his opponent. The reason to do this is that it will allow you to quickly move units from your base to your opponents along with allow you to send reinforcements to your army quicker when you are attacking your opponent.

Along with giving your units a road of creep to follow you are also giving yourself view of the entire map and the pathway to your base. This will let you know if and when your opponent is going to make a move to expand or to attack you. Where every you have a creep tumor along your Creep highway you will have sight of that area.
