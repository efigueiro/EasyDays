Extra Drone, Extractor Trick

Posted By Shokz On May 27, 2010 @ 6:13 pm In Zerg Guides | No Comments

The Drone Extractor trick is a common used trick in early game to give you an extra drone before you get your first overlord out.

As Zerg when you start the game you start producing drones to get your economy going, although you quickly will have to build and overlord before you get to 10 out of 10 supply of drones. A quick trick to get an extra drone out while your overlord is being built is the Drone Extractor trick.

How it works

To do this when you are at 10 supply take a drone and build an extractor, this will then decrease your supply count to 9 / 10 since when a drone morphs into a building that drone is then taken off your supply count. This will then allow you to quickly build an new drone to get back up to 10 supply.

Once you have the new 10th drone being built you can cancel the extractor and you will get that drone back that you used to build the extractor. This ends up giving you an extra drone before you get your first overlord out and will be at 11 / 10 supply. Once you have done this you can continue with your normal build order.

This technique is used by almost all pro Zerg players and is very common in any Zerg game.
