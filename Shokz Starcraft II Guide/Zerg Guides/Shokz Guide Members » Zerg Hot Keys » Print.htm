Zerg Hot Keys

Posted By Shokz On May 23, 2010 @ 5:56 am In Zerg Guides | No Comments

Units

Hotkeys to create units.

—–Zerg—–
Zerg units are created with Larva unless otherwise noted.
Drone – D
Queen – Q (Hatchery, Lair, Hive)
Overlord – V
Overseer – V (Overlord mutation)
Changeling – C (Overseer ability)
Zergling – Z
Baneling – E (Zergling mutation)
Hydralisk – H
Roach – R
Infestor – F
Infested Terran – T (Infestor ability)
Mutalisk – M
Corruptor – C
Brood Lord – B (Corruptor mutation)
Ultralisk – U

Unit Abilities

Hotkeys to tell units to do stuff.

—–Zerg—–
Burrow/Unburrow – W (Drone, Zergling, Baneling, Roach, Queen, Hydralisk, Infestor, Ultralisk)
Generate Creep – G (Overlord)
Stop Generating Creep – C (Overlord)
Morph to Overseer – V (Overlord)
Spawn Changeling – C (Overseer)
Morph to Baneling – E (Zergling)
Attack Structure (C) (Baneling)
Explode (X) (Baneling)
Spawn Creep Tumor – C (Queen)
Spawn Larva – R (Queen)
Transfusion – T (Queen)
Spawn Infested Terran – T (Infestor)
Neural Parasite – R (Infestor)
Fungal Growth – F (Infestor)
Corruption – C (Corruptor)
Morph to Brood Lord – B (Corruptor)

Buildings

Hotkeys to create buildings. Buildings are created by workers unless otherwise noted.

—–Zerg—–
Hatchery – H
Extractor – E
Spawning Pool – S
Evolution Chamber – V
Creep Tumor – C (Queen or an existing Creep Tumor, once per tumor)
Spine Crawler – C
Spore Crawler – W
Hydralisk Den – H
Baneling Nest – B
Roach Warren – R
Lair – L (Hatchery mutation)
Infestation Pit – I
Spire – S
Nydus Network – N
Nydus Worm – N (Nydus Network)
Hive – H (Lair mutation)
Greater Spire – G (Spire mutation)
Ultralisk Cavern – U

Building Abilities

Hotkeys to tell buildings to do stuff.

—–Zerg—–
Select Larva – S (Hatchery, Lair, Hive)
Set Worker Rally Point – W (Hatchery, Lair, Hive)
Uproot/Root – R (Spine Crawler, Spore Crawler)
Spawn Creep Tumor – C (Creep Tumor, once per tumor)
Summon Nydus Worm – N (Nydus Network)
Mutate into Lair – L (Hatchery)
Mutate into Hive – H (Lair)
Mutate into Greater Spire – G (Spire)

Upgrades/Research

In parentheses, the building that does the research

—–Zerg—–
Melee Attacks – M (Evolution Chamber)
Missile Attacks – A (Evolution Chamber)
Ground Carapace – C (Evolution Chamber)
Flyer Attack – A (Spire, Greater Spire)
Flyer Carapace – C (Spire, Greater Spire)
Metabolic Boost – E (Spawning Pool)
Adrenal Glands – A (Spawning Pool)
Glial Reconstitution – G (Roach Warren)
Tunneling Claws – T (Roach Warren)
Organic Carapace – O (Roach Warren)
Centrifugal Hooks – C (Baneling Nest)
Pneumatized Carapace – P (Lair, Hive)
Ventral Sacs – V (Lair, Hive)
Peristalsis – P (Infestor)
Pathogen Glands – G (Infestor)
Chitinous Plating – C (Ultralisk)
Anabolic Synthesis – A (Ultralisk)
