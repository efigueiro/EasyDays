Defending againts Void Rays

Posted By Shokz On May 25, 2010 @ 9:17 pm In Terran Guides | No Comments

The Void rays are a problem. It took me about 20 losses to find a Build
that could handle the void rays and still be able to transition over to handle other
Protoss units.

This consists of 3 things.

Macro: Be aware of cliffs. Dont build wall and dont build near cliffs.
Cliffs Are what makes the power of Void Rays so big. And a skilled player will
use all his voidrays over cliffs or near them.

Micro: Marines are essential for handling Void Rays. A marine without stim
Is no good against a voidray. And a overstimmed Marine suck against Voidrays.

Scouting: The initial build of a Protoss player will tell you about what strategy he is going.
Seeing the signs is important. For this reticular strat (Void Rays) the Protoss will go double
Gas. so this is something to look for. Also his amount of Gateways can be a tell. If he ha
2 Assimilators and only one gateway, this is a great tell that he is teching for Voids.

Now over to the build:

10X Supply Depot
12X Barraks
13X Gas
14X Marine
15X Uppgrade barraks (Tech Lab)
15X Uppgrade Command senter.
15-16X Second Gas

Pretty standard so far. By this time you should have a certain ideah of
what his strat is from the “Tells” when scouting his base. Gateway(s)
Assimilator(s)

As for now i wont show the amount of Scv’s
1x Marauder
1x Uppgrade Stim (Important!)
1x Barraks
1x Marauder

At this time you have to make the choise depending on your scouting/gut feeling.
Will he go voidrays or not? If you cant get into his base with an SCV look at his unit
composition. What is he spending his gas on? Is there a sentry there? How many stalkers?
(3 or more stalkers id say he wont go “fast” voidrays.)

If voidrays.

3rd Barrak
2nd Barrak uppgrade to Reactor.

Now you should have:
2x Marauders
1x Marines
1x Barrak with Teck Lab researching stim. Producing marines
1x Barrak with Reactor producing Marines
1x barrak Producing marines.

When his First void ray comes make shure that your
Stim pack is researched. If not dont attack the voidray.
Once the stim is ready. you will have about 8x-9x marines
Keep producing em. Stim Attack move attack move Towards the
Void ray. If he doesn’t run with it he looses it.

Now try this and work on it with a friend with skills in protoss
play.
