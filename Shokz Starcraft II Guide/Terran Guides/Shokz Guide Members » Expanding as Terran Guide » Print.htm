Expanding as Terran Guide

Posted By Shokz On June 3, 2010 @ 12:02 am In Terran Guides | No Comments

Knowing When to Expand

As a Terran player one of the difficult decisions is knowing when to expand. Most Terran players usually wall them selves in their base as they build up their forces, although sitting behind your wall for to long can allow your opponent to get map control and simply wear you out until you have no resources left as they continue to expand across the map and control the game.

In all games you want to keep a little pressure on your opponent and be the aggressor rather then on the defensive the entire game, if you keep the pressure on you opponent and are unable to break through his forces and take him out then you will need to match him in economy as you continue to build units.  What this means is if you see that your opponent is expanding, you either need to making a timing attack and take him out, or you must expand also to keep up with the economy.

How to Expand

Terran’s defense relies a lot on walling off their base and by building an you command center out in your natural usually leaves it open for attack, this is why as Terran players you want to always build your expansion inside your base on the edge near your expansion. By doing this you will not have to worry about defending it while it is being built and it will give you more time to get more forces to defend it one you actually do fly it out to the expansion.

Orbital Command First

In most games when you expand to your natural you want to build an Orbital Command center, rather then a Fortress as your natural expansion is right next to your base and will be easy to defend. So when you finish your Command Center in your base, build the Orbital Command Center onto it right away while still in your base this way you will have the 50 energy to use right away on a mule instead of waiting for it to build once you land the Command Center in the expansion.

Once you have your expansion setup you will want to then build your defense at your expansions choke rather then the ramp into your base. You can setup some seige tanks around your expansion with your units in front.

When expanding away from your base as Terran it’s a good idea to build a Fortress onto the Command Center instead of the Orbital Command so it has some defense if it is going to take some time for your units to get to it’s location to help defend it.
