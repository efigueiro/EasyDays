Walling off your base as Terran

Posted By Shokz On May 21, 2010 @ 8:20 pm In Terran Guides | No Comments

Walling off your base as Terran is a must in almost all games. When you wall off your base you are creating a wall at the entrance of your base so that your opponent cannot rush into your base in the early game. You want to usually wall off your base in every game except when you are playing against another Terran player then the option is up to you.

The reason you want to wall off your base against Protoss and Zerg is because if either of these two races rush you the first unit you can get with Terran are Marines and Zealots will defeat marines and zerglings can run right past your marines into your mineral line.

To wall off your base you want to build a Supply Depot, Barracks, and then another Supply Depot as seen in the picture above. The great thing about the Supply Depots is that you can lower and raise them so when you are ready to move out of your base you can lower the supply depot move your units out and then raise it back up.
