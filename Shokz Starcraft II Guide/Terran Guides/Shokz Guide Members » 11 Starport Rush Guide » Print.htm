11 Starport Rush Guide

Posted By Shokz On May 20, 2010 @ 8:40 pm In Terran Guides | No Comments

The 11 Starport rush is a build to get you a banshee the quickest time possible. It requires practice although once perfected you can get a banshee out in under 6 mins. When doing the 11 Starport rush your focus is to take out your opponents economy when you get the first banshee you should head straight for their mineral line and start taking out their works.

Build Order:

9 -- Refinery

10 -- Barracks

11 -- Factory with Tech Lab

11 -- Orbital Command

11 -- Starport

11 -- Supply Depot

The first ting you are going to want to build is your refinery as this is a very gas heavy build and you want to have 100 gas ready once your barracks is complete so you can start to build a factory right away.

Once the factory right away start to build a Starport. While the starport is being built build a tech lab on your factory as you will use this for your starport once it’s complete. Also use the extra minerals you have to build your orbital command center.

Once you start building your Starport then start building your first Supply Depot this will then give you enough supply to start pumping banshees, although you want to make sure you start this as soon as you start your Starport so that it’s finished by the time the start port is done.

Once the Starport is done you will want to lift up your Factory and Starport and swap the two so that your Starport can use the Tech Lab that was built on your factory which will save you the time to build another Tech Lab on your Starport.

Once you get your first banshee out set your rally point straight towards your opponents Mineral line, if you can get this build down and become quick at it you should be able to get a banshee out before your opponent has any Anti Air and you’ll be able to harass their minerals.

Not Giving Up

Not every time does this work although if you can transition into a normal macro build after trying this rush you still have a chance of surviving your all in attack. The replay below shows the player Endrick explain how to try to keep fighting even after your rush fails.

Countering to 11 Starport Rush

To counter an 11 Starport rush all you have to do is SCOUT. If you know your opponent is going for this build then build some AA or try to take them out early. The main issue with this build is if you get a early game rush then it is going to be very hard for you to defend as your at 11 supply for most of build and really will not have units until 5 mintues into the game.

This is why scouting is so important since if you find out what he is going you can quickly react and build some anti-air or go in for the rush against the 11 Starport buid.
