Hellion Dropship Harassment Guide

Posted By Shokz On May 22, 2010 @ 4:19 am In Terran Guides | No Comments

The Hellion Drop is a great way to harass your opponent and give you the advantage in the economy. Hellions shoot a flame out of their flame thrower that does AOE damage and is great at killing SCVs, Probes, and Drones.

Start off by building 4 -- 5 hellions and while you are doing so get a Starport and build a Medivac to use as the dropship. Load up your hellions in the Medivac and fly around your opponents base into the back by his minerals. Drop all your hellions off right in the middle of his work line and let them go to work. They will quickly dispatch any workers in their path, but be ready to quickly pick them back up with your Medivac as your opponent will be bringing his forces to backup his workers.

After you fly your Hellions out of his base keep try to do the same thing and keep the pressure on him while you continue to Macro and build your main force.
