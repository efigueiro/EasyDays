Barracks Scouting Guide

Posted By Shokz On May 27, 2010 @ 10:16 pm In Terran Guides | No Comments

A lot of times while playing Terran after your first SCV scout you won’t be able to get another unit into your enemies base to see what they are building so your stuck with the only logical option and that is to Scan them, but no! This is the last thing you will want to do early game as using your mule is more important since the mule will get you around 300 minerals.

So the best thing to do when trying to scout your opponents base mid game is to build a barracks on the side of his base and then fly it in over the base to scout. A barracks only cost 150 minerals so you will be saving 150 minerals by not scanning and a barracks has a lot of hp which will allow you to float it over their base for a long period of time before they can destroy it.

Where to Build Barracks

When you build your scouting Barracks you want to build it on the outside of their base on a side where they will not scout or see it until it’s built, although you want to build where it will have the quickest path into their base so it doesn’t have to fly a long way to reveal their builds.

Scouting

Once your barracks is complete you will want to lift off and just fly it over their base to see what units they have and what buildings they have so you can then build to counter what ever build order they have gone. A lot of times they will have a few anti air units although it won’t be enough to take down the barracks so after you’ve scouted their base and gained all the info you need then float your barracks out of there and land it on a cliff near by where you can then use it scout later in the game.
