Units

Hotkeys to create units.

—–Terran—–
In parentheses, the building or ability that creates the unit

SCV – S (Command Center, Orbital Command, Planetary Fortress)
MULE – E (Orbital Command ability)
Marine – A (Barracks)
Marauder – D (Barracks)
Reaper – E (Barracks)
Ghost – G (Barracks)
Hellion – E (Factory)
Siege Tank – S (Factory)
Thor – T (Factory)
Viking – V (Starport)
Medivac – D (Starport)
Raven – R (Starport)
Auto-Turret – T (Raven ability)
Point Defense Drone – D (Raven ability)
Banshee – E (Starport)
Battlecruiser – B (Starport)
