Ghost Sniper Shot Guide

Posted By Shokz On May 21, 2010 @ 1:20 am In Terran Guides | No Comments

The Terran Ghost is a strong unit, although is a unit that must be microed in order to be effective. The Ghost’s Sniper Round is a very powerful shot that deals 45 damage to Biological units.

When you have a couple of ghosts selected click “R” for Sniper Shot and hold down shift to allow for multiple ques of this attack. While holding down Shift your able to then click on a series of enemies and have your ghosts unleash a round of sniper shots on each one of them which will do devastating damage.
