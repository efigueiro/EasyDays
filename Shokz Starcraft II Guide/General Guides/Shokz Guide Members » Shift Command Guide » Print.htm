Shift Command Guide

Posted By Shokz On May 20, 2010 @ 8:49 am In General Guides | No Comments

One very useful skill that is important to master is the art of Shift clicking. With a unit selected, multiple orders can be used in succession (or “queued up”) by holding Shift while clicking the mouse or hotkey.  Actions involve almost anything from basic commands (ex: Move, attack, Hold position) to abilities (ex: EMP, Infested Terrans, Force Field) to changing modes. (Seige mode, Burrow, Warp Prism’s Matrix Mode).

Multiple buildings can be added to worker construction waypoints by holding Shift. The building does not have to be reselected from the build menu. This is very useful to have an SCV build multiple Supply Depots or other buildings one after another. The same thing can be done with probes.

SCVs and Probes should almost always be ordered to go back to mining after they have created a building by holding Shift and right clicking on the resources.
