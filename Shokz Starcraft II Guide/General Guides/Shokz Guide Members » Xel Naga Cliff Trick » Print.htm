Xel Naga Cliff Trick

Posted By Shokz On May 21, 2010 @ 1:07 am In General Guides | No Comments

Xel Naga Watch Towers are towers that when a unit is next to provides you with a large area of view and is a great tool to see when your opponent is about to attack. This is generally a good area to meet with your allies before you attack of to gather your forces as you prepare to hit your opponents base as it will provide you with the sight to see if your opponent is heading to your base.

Xel Naga Watch Tower Trick

The little trick with the Xel Naga watch tower requires a unit that can jump on a cliff such as a Reaper, or a Stalker with Blink. When you get the unit close enough to activate the Watch tower you can then have him jump on a cliff right next to the watch tower while keeping it activated.

The reason behind doing this is that it will allow you to keep the Watch Tower activated although keeping your off the ground where your opponent can attack him.
