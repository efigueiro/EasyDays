The Mentality of a Pro

Posted By Shokz On June 29, 2010 @ 4:22 am In General Guides | No Comments

What I want to discuss here is one of the most important things in Starcraft 2 and that is your gaming mentality. Starcrat 2 is not like most games and requires a very high mental fortitude and mental strength to not let your emotions get the best of you and to stay on top of your game. Starcraft is a video game of Chess that requires you to always be thinking a couple moves ahead and being ready to counter any move made by your opponent.

Starting Mindset

You have to go into each game thinking you are the best player in the match, even if your opponent is favored. If you go into a game thinking your outmatched then you’ve lost the game before it even starts. It doesn’t matter your opponents record, you cannot fear anyone. If you go into each game with the same mindset thinking that if you are prepared and focus then you’ll be able to take on anyone.

Rule #1
Have a general straight forward sort of sense, of knowing what your going to be doing at the end of the game. Like I want these tech units, i want these upgrades and this amount of bases. Just know what your going to be doing after you get an advantage

Rule #2
Just scout every expo, because the worst thing is losing because of a hidden expo.

Rule #3
This is probably the fastest way to lose the game. When you have a lead, you need to let that lead extend over a period of time. If you attack immediately after you beat his army, most times he will have an equal or less of a force than you in a defensive position. Just let that lead play out. But of course if it is obvious you can win, you know win but be really careful of attacking within say 5 minutes of you gaining an advantage.

Rule #4
I mean its rather simple to know that the way a player would get back in the game is to nuke your mineral line or kill of an expo with 2 or 3 units. So just make sure all your mineral lines are tight, and that you will be okay from nydus worms, or mass drops. And if your already at an advantage, you don’t need that much more of an advantage. I mean sure you want more of an advantage and harassing is good, but if you lose 4 templar, and a warp prism your kinda screwed.

Staying Focused during a Rush

A lot of times when a player gets rushed or get their economy damaged the immediately panic and lose focus on their original game plan. It is important to stay focused and level headed the entire game, try not to get to high and stay at an even level keeping your emotions in check. This will allow you to handle a rush more efficiently and get yourself quickly back on track to rebuild your economy and your base after a rush.

Taking your opponent out of their game

As important as it is to stay calm and focus while being rushed and attacked it is also important to take your opponent out of their game by rushing them and damaging their economy. Most non-pro players will get frustrated and lose their psychological mind set after being attacked or losing a large part of their economy. It is important to take them out of their game early so they can’t focus on their normal strategy and build.

Winning Games

Winning is the ultimate goal and although when you win you want to make sure you don’t start getting cocky, and thinking that your are invincible. Stay down to earth and take on each game the same as the next, if you start thinking your really good then you may stray from your normal strategy. I’m not saying don’t be proud of your wins, enjoy each win but know that there is always someone better than you out their that you need to beat next.

Losing Games

Losing is not something I can ever get use to doing, although it is part of the game and everyone is going to lose. Although Losing is one of the best things that can happen to you, since you can learn something new after each loss. Every time you lose you should reflect upon the game and think what you did wrong, and what you could of done that might of given you the advantage and turned the game in your favor. I

After every loss I go back and watch the reply and see what I can improve on, it is important to not get to upset after a loss and try to learn from it and improve on your weakness the next game.

Tips

  • Stay focused during rushes, don’t panic and focus on what you must do to recover
  • Do not Rage! Everyone is going to lose, learn from your loses and try to improve the next game
  • Watch your own Replays. You’ll learn a lot from watching your own replays
  • Try to take your opponent out of his game psychologically

Remember the most important thing! Have fun, this is why your playing the game.
