Controlling the Map

Posted By Shokz On June 30, 2010 @ 3:11 am In General Guides | No Comments

Controlling the map should be a focus point of every game. When a player has control of the map they have control over the economy and almost always wins the game. In this guide I’m going to talk about the advantages of having map control, how to keep it, and what to do once you have map control.

What is Map Control

Map Control is where you control the map and all the expansions of the map with your army. By having map control your able to deny your opponent from expanding or letting him take control of any additional expansions. The player who has map control, controls the speed of the game as they can decide to expand and build up their army or move in on their opponent. It is very critical to not let your opponent gain control of the map because you will then be cornered in your base forced to attack when your not ready.

Gaining Map Control

Gaining Control over the map is all about having a mobile army, you cannot sit back in your base base massing forces waiting to attack, you need to have your army out on the map not letting your opponent expand past his natural expansion. When taking map be aware of your opponents force, if you try to deny an expansion or control the map when you are out numbered or matched in army size you can lose the game.

Attacking with Map Control

When you have map control you need to know when to attack and when to sit back and continue to Macro, because if you attack at the wrong time you can lose the battle and let your opponent gain control over the map. The key is to keep pressure on your opponent by constantly hitting him and if your not able to push into his base and win then pull back and continue to macro and then apply more pressure on him soon after.

Eventually when you have map control you’ll be able to push through their forces and win the game, although make sure that you keep up the unit production so that you don’t lose a main battle turning the control in their favor.

Let your opponent Turtle

A Turtle is a player who will build a wall of defense at the front of their base or at the front of their expansion making it difficult to attack them while they sit back and tech. When you are facing a turtle they key to the game is slowly bleed them out of their minerals. With a turtle you will have complete control over the map since they will not come out of their base and this is where you need to expand across the map to gain control over the economy.

A Turtle is going to be making a timing attack waiting till he is ready to hit so you must have a balance of expanding and building forces so that you will be able to defend off any timing attack. Make sure that you are constantly scouting a turtle so that you know what he is going and when you think he will be ready to attack. When trying to scout a turtle you won’t be able to send a unit straight into a base so fly and observer, overlord or a barracks into their base to get the scouting info.

As long as you hold of any timing attack of the turtle and deny him any further expansions past their natural you will slowly win the game, these games are long and tiring but you will bleed him dry of resources as you continue to expand and macro your army if you deny him from expanding.
