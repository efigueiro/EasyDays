Mining & Gathering Guide

Posted By Shokz On May 27, 2010 @ 5:14 pm In General Guides | No Comments

Economy

Your economy is the gathering of minerals and gas, in most games the player who has the best economy has an advantage in the game and is a very important part to keeping a good Macro.

Saturating Minerals

The term Saturating Minerals means adding the optimal amount of workers (SCVs, Probes, Drones) to your mineral line. To earn the most of your minerals you should have 3 workers per mineral patch and there are 8 minerals patches in a normal base so you should have 24 workers on minerals.

The same goes for Gas, you should have 3 workers harvesting gas per geyser. Most build orders have you get your gas early in the game, so make sure that you add 3 workers right away harvest from it.

Many times as the game goes on and you start building units, players will forget to continue to make workers and saturate their minerals. This is where having your main set as Control Group Hot key so you can quickly hit the number to select it and then build a worker while you continue to Macro your base.

As the game goes on and the time comes to expand your main minerals will start to be getting low, instead of building a all new workers for your expansion take half of the workers off your main and have them start harvesting from your expansion so you don’t have to build as many workers and the other half will finish harvesting whatever minerals remains from your main base.

High Yield Minerals

High Yield Minerals are the gold mineral patches which you can find usually in the middle of over map. These minerals give you an economic edge as your workers are able to gather minerals at a faster rate allowing you to produce more units.

The high yield minerals only have 6 mineral patches compared to the normal minerals 8 patches, but they provide a large increase in resource gathering. A worker gathers 5 minerals per trip from a normal mineral patch, where they gather 7 minerals per trip from a high yield mineral patch. That is a 40% increase in mineral mining which is huge jump to your economy.

As stated these high yield mineral patches are usually found around the center of the map and should be utilized as often as possible. They key is to have map control and have an active army so your able to expand quickly and protect your expansion. If a player takes control of a high yield mineral they will have a huge advantage in the game as their economy will be soaring above their opponents.

Vespine Gas

Vespine Gas is the other resource that is gathered in Starcraft 2, to start gathering gas you need to create an your races gas collecting building on a Vespine Geyser and then you’ll be able to start gathering gas. There are two Vespine Geysers per base and like minerals you want to have 3 workers gathering gas per geyser.

There is no difference between Vespine Geysers at high yield minerals or at a normal base, they both gather the same amount of gas and it takes 3 workers to saturate each geyser.
