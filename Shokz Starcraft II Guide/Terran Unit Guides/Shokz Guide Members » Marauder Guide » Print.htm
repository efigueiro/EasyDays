Marauder Guide

Posted By Shokz On July 15, 2010 @ 3:13 am In Terran Unit Guides | No Comments

Race Weak Against Okay Against Strong Against
Zerg Brood Lord, Mutalisk, Hydralisk, Zergling Baneling Roach, Ultralisk
Protoss Carrier, Mothership, Void Ray, Dark Templar, Immortal, Zealot Colossus, High Templar, Sentry Stalker
Terran Banshee, Battlecruiser, Siege Tank Ghost, Hellion, Marine Thor, Viking, Reaper

The Marauder is Terran’s armored ground unit which attacks only ground units, aka Black Rocket Man. Marauders are a great addition to the Terran infantry as they are a very powerful ground unit that in numbers can be very powerful. They work great in a combination of marines which is known as M&M Ball which is used as a great rush and very powerful against any combination of any unit.

Marauder Upgrades

Stimpack: is an upgraded that is researched from the Tech Lab, which increases the movement speed and the firing rate for both marines and marauders for 15 seconds at the cost of HP. Each time you cast stimpack it reduces the HP of the marine by 10 but is very useful in battle as it basically doubles your damage output and movement speed.

  • Stimpacks are great to use at the start of a battle, as you will get the most out of the 15 seconds and help deal out the most amount of damage.
  • Stimpacks do not stack, casting it twice in a row will only make you lose an additional 10 HP and not give you any additional benefit.
  • When casting Stimpack a green lightning bolt will appear and once Stimpack fads you will see a red lightning bolt above their head.

Concussive Shells: is an upgrade that is also researched from the Tech Lab which allows the marauder to slow the movement speed of any unit by 50% on attack. This is a great ability and should be researched any time you are going to go a heavy marauder based army.

  • Concussive Shells allows you to kite melee units like Zealots and Roaches
  • You can chase down enemies as they flee Stimpack and Concussive Shells to slow them down and kill them

Against Other Races

Vs Protoss: Marauders are great against almost all Protoss units, they are able to kite Zealots when they have concussive shells and are very strong against stalkers and Immortals. The one unit you have to watch out for is Colossus, when you see your opponent has them you will need to Stim up and focus fire them down quickly as they will eat the marauders up very quickly. It is also important to scout Protoss a lot to make sure they are not getting Void Rays as marauders against any air with no backup are dead in the water.

Vs Zerg: Marauders are a great unit against Zerg as well as they are very strong vs Roaches and are good against Hydralisks. The main worry you need to watch out for is zerglings as they are very week against them although with marines or hellions as backup they can dispatch a group of zerglings very quickly, also watch out for Mutalisks which you would then need to back up your marauders with anti air such as Mainres, Thors or Vikings.

Vs Terran: Marauders against Terran are very dependent on what your Terran opponent is going as most Terran players eventually lead to a Thor / Siege Tank build or Air build this makes Marauders of little important in a Terran based game, unless you are rushing. They are a great unit to rush early game against Terran before they get Siege Tanks, or Air and are also great at defending against Reaper rush once Concussive Shells is researched.
