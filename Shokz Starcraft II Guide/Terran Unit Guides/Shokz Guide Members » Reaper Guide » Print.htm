Reaper Guide

Posted By Shokz On July 17, 2010 @ 3:11 pm In Terran Unit Guides | No Comments

Race Weak Against Okay Against Strong Against
Zerg Ultralisk, Baneling, Brood Lord, Roach Hydralisk Zergling
Protoss Colossus, High Templar, Archon, Zealot, Stalker Dark Templar, High Templar, Sentry Zealot
Terran Banshee, Marauder, Siege Tank, Thor, Viking Ghost, Hellion, Marine

Reapers are a unique unit with the ability to jump up and off cliffs, and move quickly across the map. They are light weight unit which is very weak when being attacked which is why they are mainly just used as a harassment unit to try to damage their employs economy. They can also be used to kite melee units such as zealots and zerglings by hitting-and-running. They have a secondary attack where they throw grenades at buildings which causes a good amount of damage.

Reaper Skills and Strategies

Nitro Pack: Is a Reaper upgrade trained from the Tech Lab of a barracks, which increases the movement speed of your reapers. The upgrade cost 50 minerals an 50 vespene and is worth the upgrade if you plan to do a heavy Reaper rush or harassment.

  • Reaper Rush Guide
  • When Harassing your main target should be to take out their workers, try to damage their economy
  • Don’t use the front door! Reapers can jump up and off cliffs so sneak into your opponents base through the side or back.

Against Other Races

Vs Protoss: When going against Protoss you want to try to kill as many probes as possible, if your opponent gets zealots you will be able to kite them around his base by hitting them and running. Once the Protoss gets a stalker then your days are numbered and you should focus on getting as many drones as possible before the stalker takes you out.

Vs Zerg: It is tough to take on Zerg with Reapers, as Zerglings on creep can quickly catch up and surround your Reapers, and zerglings with their Metablic Speed upgrade can catch them when they are off creep also. Although one strategy that works very well is when a Zerg player goes for a quick expansion you can take an SCV and build a Bunker next to his expansion and put 2 – 3 Reapers in the bunker which will dispose of any zerglings defending the expansion and then quickly take out the expansion with their grenades which they throw at buildings.

Vs Terran: Attacking Terran with Reapers is a lot like Protoss, your main focus will to kill as many SCVs as possible and your able to kite marines, although once they get a marauder then they will easily be able to defend their minerals so getting your reapers out ASAP is crucial when attacking another Terran player.
