Siege Tank Guide

Posted By Shokz On July 15, 2010 @ 3:49 am In Terran Unit Guides | No Comments

Race Weak Against Okay Against Strong Against
Zerg Brood Lord, Mutalisk, Zergling Baneling, Roach, Ultralisk Hydralisk
Protoss Carrier, Mothership, Phoenix, Void Ray, Dark Templar, Zealot Archon, Colossus, High Tempar. Sentry, Stalker
Terran Banshee, Battlecruiser Hellion, Marauder, Reaper, Viking, Thor Marine, Ghost

Siege Tanks are Terran’s ranged ground unit that is built from a factory with a Tech Lab. You must research the siege tech first in order to use the siege mode which allows your tank to siege up and deal a great amount of damage with very long range, but becomes unmovable.

Against other Races

Vs Protoss: Tanks are a great unit against most Protoss builds. They are very strong against Stalkers and Zealots as long as they are shooting them from a distance, you have to watch out for stalkers blinking onto your tanks and zealots charging up to them, although if you have some Marauders or thors in front of them you should be fine.The one unit you have to be careful of is Immortals, they have a shield that reflects the tank’s damage, but you can EMP them to take their shield off.

Vs Terran: I go tanks almost every time I plan against another Terran player, unless I plan on going an Air based build. Tanks are useful against every Terran unit and can take out Marine Marauder combination very quickly.

Vs Zerg: Just like with Terran tanks are very strong against most Zerg units as long as you keep them at a distance. They will qucikly dispose of Hydras and Zerglings if you don’t let them get to close. They are also very useful in stopping a Banling bust, by placing them behind bases entrance wall to stop any upcoming Banlings.
