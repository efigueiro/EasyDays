Thor Guide

Posted By Shokz On July 17, 2010 @ 3:52 pm In Terran Unit Guides | No Comments

Race Weak Against Okay Against Strong Against
Zerg Zergling, Baneling Brood Lord, Hydralisk, Mutalisk Roach, Ultralisk
Protoss Immortal, Zealot Carrier, Void Ray, Dark Templar, High Templar, Stalker Archon, Colossus, Mothership, Sentry
Terran Banshee, Marauder, Marine, Siege Tank Battlecruiser, Ghost, Hellion Reaper, Viking

“Thor is Here”! Thors are a powerful high end unit which can attack both air and ground and has a ton of hit points. They are a decent unit for anti-air and a very powerful unit when being backed up by siege tanks and marauders.

Skills and Strategies

250mm Strike Cannons: This allows the thor to fire at a single unit or structure, stunning the target and dealing 500 damage over 6 seconds. This is a very powerful ability that can be used when dealing with other high end units such as Ultralisk, Battlecruisers, or Colossus to take them down quickly. You can also use the attack to try to quickly take down buildings during a Thor drop or when invading their base.

  • During battle use a couple SCVs to heal Thors while being attacked.
  • You can call down a mule anywhere on the map where you Thors are to heal them.
  • Use 250mm Strike Cannon to quickly take down opponents Nexus, Command Center, or Hive.

Against other Races

Vs Protoss: Thors are not the strongest unit to go against Protoss, since Immortals can quickly dispose of them along with zealots. Thors are a decent match up if your opponent is going a very heavy stalker build but if he is more zealots and immortals then stay away from Thors. You can also build Thors if you plan on doing a Thor Drop as a harassment but in a straight battle they are not the best idea.

Vs Terran: Thors work well against other Teran players since you can use their cannons to quickly take out their Siege tanks and when teamed up your own Siege tanks they provide great meat to protect your tanks from being focused fired on.

Vs Zerg: This is where I tend to usually get Thors the most is when I play against Zerg. Thors are very strong against Hydralisks since they are able to 1 shot them and are very good at defending against Mutalisk rushes. Thors do splash damage against air and a couple of Thors can easily take out a group of Mutalisks when they are grouped together. The main thing you have to watch out for are Zerglings, they are very quick and if they surround a Thor they will quickly take him down with, if you are going a heavy Thor build try to have them backed up by a couple Hellions so they can take out any Zerglings that try to surround your Thors.
