Banshee Guide

Posted By Shokz On July 13, 2010 @ 3:11 pm In Terran Unit Guides | No Comments

Race Weak Against Okay Against Strong Against
Zerg Corruptor, Mutalisk, Hydralisk Queen Baneling, Roach, Ultralisk, Zergling
Protoss Carrier, Mothership, Phoenix, Void Ray, Archon, Sentry, Stalker, High Templar Colossus, Dark Templar, Immortal, Zealot
Terran Battlecruiser, Viking, Thor Marine, Ghost Hellion, Marauder, Reaper, Siege Tank

Banshees are light armored air units which can only attack ground and has the ability to be cloaked with an upgrade. The Banshee is a very popular unit to air rush with due to the cloak ability and is very good at harassing and killing workers if your opponent doesn’t have any detection.

Skills and Strategies

Cloak: This skill Cloaks the Banshee, preventing enemy units from seeing or attacking it. A cloaked unit will only be revealed by detector units or effects. This is very useful in attacking opponents economy, a couple expansions can quickly take out an entire force of workers and if your opponent forget to get any detection you can take out his entire army also.

  • 11 Starport Rush Guide
  • Take out any detection units first such as Photon Cannons / Turrets
  • Hide your Starport away from your main structures this way if your opponent scouts he may not see the Starport and not expect banshees
  • Use Vikings to backup your group of Banshees, the Vikings will take out any air units while the Banshees will handle the ground units

Against other Races

Vs Protoss: Banshees are very powerful support unit against Protoss, when mixed in your army and cloaked they are able to do a great amount of damage while their only threat in Stalkers. You have to watch out for Void Rays, although if they go that route you can then start massing Vikings.

Vs Terran: Terran players have the ability to Scan which allows them to have detection any time they need it as long as they have the energy, which makes banshees less useful against Terran players. You can use them to harass their minerals and once they bring units over and scan then fly away, but against a good player they will easily be able to defend it. In the lower level leagues they should work quite well.

Vs Zerg: Banshees are good against Zerg if you rush them with them. Zerg doesn’t really get any anti-air until they make their Liar and then Hydralisks so if you get banshees out while the Zerg player still has only Roaches or Zerlings you will be very well off, but once he has Hydralisks he will easily be able to defend them off.
