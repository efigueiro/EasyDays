Hellion Guide

Hellions are the new Firebats in Starcraft 2 in form of a Vulture, they are a fast moving vehicles with a flame thrower on top that burns the enemies. They are a great unit for scouting due to their extremely fast speed and are a great harassing unit, a couple of Hellions will quickly destroy workers mining minerals.

Hellion Upgrades

Infernal Pre-Igniter: This is an upgraded from the factories Tech Lab which improves the flame thrower to give +10 damage vs light armored units. This is a great upgrade if you are facing a player who is going a heavy zergling, or zealot build as this is where hellions excel at.

Against other Races

Vs Protoss: Hellions are great against Zealots, if your opponent is going a heavy Zealot built then going a mix of Marauders and Hellions will be a great choice as the Marauders will provide the meat for the hellions as they use their range and burn down the zealots from behind.

Vs Zerg: Just like Zealots, Hellions are able to easily dispose of Zerglings when they are grouped up. As long as the Zerglings don’t get a sorround on the Hellions you’ll be ok, make sure to have a mixture of units with the hellions to take out the Zerglings such as Marauders. Hellions are also a good choice for Hydralisk too, a Marauder and Hellion combo is one of the best choices when going against Zerglings and Hdyras.

Vs Terran: Hellions are good when going up against a large marine based army, a lot of times in a Terran vs Terran game the player will no wall off their base which will allow you to easily run 3 – 4 hellions into the back of their base and start taking down their SCVs.
