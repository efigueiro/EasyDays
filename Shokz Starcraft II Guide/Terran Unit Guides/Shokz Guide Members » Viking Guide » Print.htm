Viking Guide

Posted By Shokz On July 16, 2010 @ 3:39 am In Terran Unit Guides | No Comments

Race Weak Against Okay Against Strong Against
Zerg Hydralisk, Zergling Baneling, Corruptor, Roach, Ultralisk Brood Lord, mutalisk, Overseer
Protoss Immortal, Stalker Archon, Dark Templar, High Templar, Sentry Carrier, Colossus, Mothership, Void Ray
Terran Marauder, Marine, Siege Tank Ghost, Hellion, Reaper, Thor Banshee, Battlecruiser

Vikings are Terran’s main anti-air unit and an all around very versatile unit in the Terran army. The Viking has two attack modes Fighting Mode which is their air based mode and Assault Mode which is their ground attack mode. This allows the viking to be used in many situations but their main purpose and where they excel is in their Fighting Mode being used as anit-air. They have very long range while in their flying mode allowing them to snipe out other air units and are a good counter to almost every air unit in the game.

Skills and Strategies

Fighter Mode: Transforms the Viking into Fighter Mode. In this mode Vikings fly, but they can only attack air targets. While in Fighting mode the Viking can out range almost any other air unit and is the best choice for anti-air when playing as Terran.

Assault Mode: Transforms the Viking to Assault Mode. In this mode Vikings move on the ground and can only attack ground targets. This is very useful in battles if you need a little extra ground support and can help turn the tide of the battle. Vikings in Assault mode are not the strongest unit and should not be made for this purpose but as secondary feature to be used when needed.

  • Use Vikings to find opponent expansions and then drop down into Assault mode behind the mineral line to take out their workers.
  • Use Vikings to harass opponents expansions and main mineral fields
  • While in Fighting mode take out Overlords around the map to try to supply block your Zerg opponent.

Against other Races

Vs Protoss: Vikings can be very useful in a Protoss match-up, they are the best counter to Colossus since Air units can attack Colossus since they are so tall and with the Vikings long range they are able to shoot them from a distance since Colossus are usually behind the Protoss forces. Another great use for Vikings are when your opponent goes for Void Rays, as Vikings are the best Terran counter for Void Rays and can out range them to keep them at a distance.

Vs Terran: Vikings are good in Terran games to harass since a lot of players will build expansions and fly them away off to an island so you will easily be able to fly a couple Vikings over to their expansion, take Assault mode and take out their SCVs. Vikings are also very useful against any type of Banshee build, since Banshees can only attack ground a couple Vikings will easily take out a group of Banshees.

Vs Zerg: Vikings are a decent counter to Mutalisks and is usually the only time you would want to get vikings in a Zerg game. You can use Vikings to pick off overlords scouting the map or find the group of their Overlord supply and take them out to take them below the supply count but other than that Vikings aren’t used a lot in a Zerg match-up. If the game ever goes into a late game and your opponent starts to go Brood Lords then Vikings will be the best counter, although it’s rare for games to go that far.
